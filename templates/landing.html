How it works

From zero to perfect route
in four steps

1
Pick your start & destination
Choose from 8 real attractions across the city — or tap "Use My Location" and GPS auto-selects the nearest one as your starting point.
2
Set your transport & priority
Choose car, bike, bus, or walking. Then tell RouteWise whether you care most about distance, cost, or time — and it optimises for exactly that.
3
Get your live-enriched route
The algorithm runs Dijkstra's over the city graph while pulling live traffic and weather for every road segment. High-traffic roads show bypass alternatives instantly.
4
Follow the map & explore
Your route appears on an interactive map with your GPS dot moving in real time. Every scenic waypoint is marked, and the full cost breakdown is itemised to the last rupee.
